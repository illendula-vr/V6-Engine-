# V6 Engine CAD Design Project

## Overview

This project showcases the complete 3D modeling and assembly of a V6 Internal Combustion Engine developed using SolidWorks. The objective was to gain practical experience in complex mechanical assemblies, engine architecture, component integration, and engineering design principles.

The project includes detailed modeling of major engine components, assembly verification, rendering, and engineering documentation.

---

## Project Objectives

- Design a complete V6 engine assembly.
- Develop accurate 3D models of engine components.
- Understand engine kinematics and power transmission mechanisms.
- Practice large assembly management and constraint application.
- Generate professional engineering documentation.

## Major Components

### Engine Block
Main structural component housing the cylinders and crankshaft.

### Cylinder Heads
Designed to accommodate valves, ports, and combustion chambers.

### Pistons
Modeled to reciprocate within the cylinders and transfer force.

### Connecting Rods
Connect pistons to the crankshaft and convert linear motion into rotational motion.

### Crankshaft
Central rotating component responsible for power transmission.

### Camshaft
Controls intake and exhaust valve timing.

### Flywheel
Provides rotational inertia and smooth engine operation.

### Intake and Exhaust Manifolds
Designed for air intake and exhaust gas flow management.
